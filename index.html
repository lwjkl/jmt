May 24 – June 2, 2026
May 24Today Sunday
Atami Fireworks Event
Meet at Odawara, drop bags at Airbnb, then train to Atami for the museum and fireworks over the bay.
  • ~14:00 — Meet at Odawara station
  • 15:00 – 16:30 — MOA Museum of Art
  • 17:00 – 19:00 — Atami town, dinner near Sun Beach
  • 20:20 – 20:40 — Atami Marine Fireworks
  • ~21:30 — Train back to Odawara
Tip: Buy return train tickets before fireworks. Atami station gets packed — let the surge pass at a café for 30 min.
May 25 – 28Today Mon – Thu
Weather-Driven Days Flexible
Car pickup May 25 · 08:00. Check Fuji visibility each morning, then pick the bundle. Wake 05:00, depart ~06:00, return ~19:00. Hakone is the safe default for Day 1.
⛩ Hakone Loop
Easy · 40 min from Odawara · safe Day 1 pick
  • Hakone Shrine + Heiwa-no-Torii
  • Owakudani (volcanic vents, black eggs)
  • Old Tōkaidō Cedar Avenue
  • Hakone Ashinoko Observation Park
  • Hakone Gora Park (optional)
☀ Fuji Five Lakes
Pick on a clear Fuji day · ~2hr from Odawara
  • Arakurayama Sengen Park (Chureito Pagoda · 398 steps)
  • Kanadorii (gold torii)
  • Oshino Hakkai ponds
  • Lake Yamanaka panorama
🌊 Izu — Hard Day
Depart 05:00 · long drive south
  • Shimoda Park (Shiroyama)
  • Shirahama Beach (weather permitting)
  • Kawazu Seven Waterfalls
  • Joren Falls
🏞 Izu — Easy Day
Scenic ridge driving
  • Izu Skyline drive
  • Mt. Komuro Ridge Walk "Misora"
  • Mt. Omuro (chairlift to crater rim)
  • Jogasaki Coast + Kadowaki Suspension Bridge
May 29Today Friday
Buffer + Manazuru Light
Backup for a missed bundle, otherwise Cape Manazuru. Return car by 18:30.
  • Cape Manazuru (20 min from Odawara)
  • Late lunch in Odawara
  • Return car by 18:30 sharp
May 30Today Saturday
Move to Tokyo · Adachi Fireworks Event
Early train from Odawara (~2hr) to drop luggage, then shopping around Tokyo Station / Shinjuku, Tokyo National Museum at Ueno, then Arakawa for fireworks.
  • Early morning — Pack out, train Odawara → Tokyo (~2hr)
  • Late morning — Drop luggage at Bay Hotel, Urayasu
  • Midday — Shopping · Tokyo Station or Shinjuku
  • Afternoon — Tokyo National Museum (Ueno)
  • ~17:00 — Head to Arakawakosuge Park
  • 19:20 – 20:20 — Adachi Fireworks
Heads-up: 700K attendees. Arrive by 17:00 to find a spot. Exit via Machiya, Senju-Ohashi, or Adachi-Odai — not Kita-Senju.
May 31Today Sunday
Yokohama Day
Harbor walks, traditional garden, museum. End at Osanbashi Pier for the night skyline. More breathing room than Kamakura on a weekend.
  • Sankeien Garden
  • Future Rose Garden (Yamashita Park)
  • Red Brick Warehouse + harbor
  • Yokohama Port Museum
  • Yokohama Air Cabin (Sakuragicho ropeway)
  • Harbor View Park Observatory
  • Osanbashi Pier — sunset & night view
June 1Today Monday
Kamakura + Enoshima
Weekday = much quieter. Temples, shopping street, and seaside.
  • Hase Temple
  • Komachi-dori Shopping Street
  • Meigetsu-in
  • Enoshima (sea breeze, sunset)
Pending: Tsurugaoka Hachimangu — add if time allows (sits at the end of Komachi-dori, ~5 min walk).
June 2Today Tuesday
Departure
Half day for souvenirs and a city walk. Be at Haneda by 20:30 for 23:30 flight.
  • Morning — Souvenirs, light city walk
  • ~17:00 — Pack and check out
  • ~19:00 — Leave Urayasu for Haneda
  • 23:30 — Flight to KLIA
🚶 Tokyo city walk picks
Choose any · all near central Tokyo
  • Tokyo Station Marunouchi Building (architecture)
  • Bank of Japan Head Office (Meiji-era façade)
  • Tsukiji Hongan-ji Temple (Indian-inspired)
  • The National Art Center, Tokyo (Roppongi)
Plan subject to weather and group energy
