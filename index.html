Arrival

May 24 Sunday
Atami Fireworks Event
Meet at Odawara, drop bags at Airbnb, then train to Atami for the museum and fireworks over the bay.
  • ~14:00 — Meet at Odawara station
  • 15:00 – 16:30 — MOA Museum of Art
  • 17:00 – 19:00 — Atami town, dinner near Sun Beach
  • 20:20 – 20:40 — Atami Marine Fireworks
  • ~21:30 — Train back to Odawara

May 29 Friday
Buffer + Manazuru Light

  • Late lunch in Odawara
  • Return car by 18:30 sharp
May 30 Saturday
Move to Urayasu · Adachi Fireworks Event
Train from Odawara, drop luggage at Bay Hotel first, then Ueno, then Arakawa for fireworks.
  • Morning — Pack out, train to Urayasu
  • Midday — Drop luggage at Bay Hotel
  • Afternoon — Ueno Park, Shinobazu Pond
  • ~17:00 — Head to Arakawakosuge Park
  • 19:20 – 20:20 — Adachi Fireworks
Heads-up: 700K attendees. Arrive by 17:00 to find a spot. Exit via Machiya, Senju-Ohashi, or Adachi-Odai — not Kita-Senju.
May 31 Sunday
Yokohama Day
Harbor walks, traditional garden, museum. End at Osanbashi Pier for the night skyline. More breathing room than Kamakura on a weekend.
  • Sankeien Garden
  • Future Rose Garden (Yamashita Park)
  • Red Brick Warehouse + harbor
  • Yokohama Port Museum
  • Yokohama Air Cabin (Sakuragicho ropeway)
  • Harbor View Park Observatory
  • Osanbashi Pier — sunset & night view
June 1 Monday
Kamakura + Enoshima
Weekday = much quieter. Temples and seaside.
  • Hase Temple
  • Tsurugaoka Hachimangu
  • Meigetsu-in
  • Enoshima (sea breeze, sunset)
June 2 Tuesday
Departure
Half day for souvenirs and a city walk. Be at Haneda by 20:30 for 23:30 flight.
  • Morning — Souvenirs, light city walk
  • ~17:00 — Pack and check out
  • ~19:00 — Leave Urayasu for Haneda
  • 23:30 — Flight to KLIA
